About the Work

EconLab

Econlab is a series of projects trying to demonstrate and explore idiosyncracies of economics, starting with explorations of supply and demand.

VizFin

VizFin is a series of projects helping users understand a company's finances in a visual way.

These were mostly borne out of attempts to understand the CFA curriculum without memorizing formulas.

About the Author

I've always been overwhelmed by the sheer complexity of the world, which probably explains my interest in economics, statistics, and computer science -- these being disciplines that share a common purpose in allowing humans to understand and control large and messy real-world problems.

After graduating from the University of Pennsylvania with degrees in computer science and economics, I worked at Goldman Sachs in risk management, and then The Rockefeller University, where I helped invest the school's endowment portfolio.

Believing that the majority of investment returns are driven by macroeconomics, and that most investors don't have the tools or time to address macroeconomics, I set out to create a startup (Becon.ai) that would bring the tools necesary for understanding macroeconomics, quickly and easily, to consumers.
